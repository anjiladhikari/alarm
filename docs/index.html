Privacy Policy for Alarm

Effective date: 16 July 2026

Alarm is provided by Green Rhino. This privacy policy explains how the Alarm mobile application handles user information.

Information collection

Alarm does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information. The app does not require users to create an account or sign in.

Information stored on the device

Alarm details, including alarm times and labels, are stored locally on the user's device. This information is used only to provide the app's alarm functionality and is not transmitted to the developer or third parties.

Permissions

The app may request notification and exact-alarm permissions. These permissions are used only to schedule and display alarms at the times selected by the user.

Advertising and analytics

Alarm does not contain advertising and does not use third-party advertising or analytics services.

Children's privacy

Alarm is not specifically designed or directed toward children under 13, and the app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated if the app's features or data practices change. Updates will be published on this page.
